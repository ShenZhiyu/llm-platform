# NIST AI Risk Management Framework Summary

Source reference:
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0: https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework

This local knowledge-base note summarizes public NIST AI RMF guidance for teams evaluating AI systems.

The NIST AI Risk Management Framework is organized around functions that help organizations govern, map, measure, and manage AI risks. It is intended to be voluntary, flexible, and usable across sectors and AI system types.

The Govern function focuses on policies, accountability, roles, documentation, and organizational processes. The Map function identifies context, intended use, stakeholders, benefits, risks, and potential impacts. The Measure function evaluates, analyzes, and tracks risks using qualitative and quantitative methods. The Manage function prioritizes and responds to risks throughout the AI lifecycle.

The framework encourages continuous risk management rather than a one-time checklist. It highlights characteristics such as validity, reliability, safety, security, resilience, accountability, transparency, explainability, privacy, and fairness.
